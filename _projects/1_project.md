The Qiskit Summer School lectures have recently been uploaded to [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLOFEBzvs-VvqoeIypXYLLf0PY-WOQMLR3), so I can only recommend you check them out!
This project's goal is to gain an advanced knowledge about quantum computation, to get a full understanding of the fundamentals and to find inspiration for my own quantum projects.
To achieve this goal, I will write an extended summary of each lecture on my [journal](http://localhost:8080/blog/) (extended in the sense that I will dig deeper into topics that were only named in the lecture). This will
* make me remember it easier
* let me track my progress
* give you a summary of the summer school, if you can't spend 16 hours of your time for the lectures

I will probably post each new journal entry quite irregularly, but what I am planning is to upload one post per week.
