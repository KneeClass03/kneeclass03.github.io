Welcome to my personal sandbox of ideas, goals and achievements. I hope you will benefit from it.

I am a student of computer science, with a wide-ranging field of interest. However, what I am pretty confident of is that I am fascinated by quantum computers. You ask why?

Firstly, the current state of quantum computing is referred to as the **Noisy intermediate-scale quantum** (NISQ) era, which basically means that quantum computers are still too error-prone and small to have any advantage in using them. Leading experts predict that this era will end in between 5 to 20 years; after this era, quantum computers will be able to use algorithms that no classical computer was ever able to use.
This seems unbelievable to me, regarding the huge change that quantum computing will bring to our world. For example, did you now that TLS, the encryption standard for HTTPS, will most probably be decryptable by quantum computers in the future? The whole web will be decrytable, and no one is prepared for it.
I want to be involved in shaping this new technological era, with respect to data privacy and for the sake of society. The applications of QC are numerous, and I am excited to witness the many scientific breakthroughs in conjunction with this technology.

Now that was a long first argument...

Secondly, I am big fan of gate-based quantum computers and SDKs like [Qiskit](https://qiskit.org), as they combine my interests in software development and data science with my interests in computer hardware. Working with gates inside a Jupyter notebook, what could be better!?

Now that was enough of an introduction, better head over to my blog to read more about my activities or take a look at the projects that I am working on!
